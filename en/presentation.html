A. The area of the activity: maritime transport

With 9 billion tons of freight per year, the seaway is the primary mode of freight transportation and occupies a growing place in international trade. This is an inexpensive way but the routing times are longer. Goods can be transported on specialized polytherm vessels intended for the transport of perishable foodstuffs or on non-specialized vessels (conventional freighters, container ships, mixed vessels, barge carriers for transport combined river / sea).

Containerization allows the lowest costs and offers a number of advantages such as cold chain security, traceability, timeliness, services and door to door deliveries. Technological progress linked to packaging, mechanization, refrigeration of ships (products under controlled atmosphere and under modified atmosphere), the tracking of goods via computerized systems enabled for maritime transport to become competitive in the transport of fresh products, resulting in lower costs.

The refrigerated freight transported around the world relates mainly to meat, fish, dairy products, fruits and vegetables (including 80% bananas, citrus fruits and others...). To transport them, we use reefer ships (a refrigerated vessel), the most economical and least polluting mode. However, he is competing with containerization which benefits from the growth of product exchanges costs and preferences of customers (including mass distribution) sensitive to prices and just-in-time supplies.

However, the lack of flexibility in port infrastructure (ports of loading / unloading) and its high cost are all obstacles to the growth of this economy.

However, the favorable economic context (globalization of consumer markets, liberalization of trade) perpetuates the place of maritime transport in trade.

B. Tholdi company

THOLDI is specialized in the management of containers intended for the transport of goods. It acts as a service provider on behalf of transport companies but since 2010, it has also been developing a freight activity through its subsidiary “Eole”.

THOLDI's head office is located in the Paris region and its areas of activity are located in several European port facilities:

  • Gennevilliers (FR),
  • Havre (FR),
  • Marseille (FR),
  • Hambourg (DE),
  • Anvers (BL),
  • Barcelone (ES),
  • Rotterdam (NL).

D. Missions and professions

Its main activity consists of:

  • Manage the unloading and reception of containers (control of their origin and of the maritime transporter) ;
  • Manage placement in temporary storage area ;
  • Manage container loading on road transport or rail transport trailers ;
  • Manage the door-to-door freight forwarding process.

THOLDI is also equipped with repair workshops for containers (Dry, Reefer, etc.) and offers container maintenance and repair services to its customers and partners.
